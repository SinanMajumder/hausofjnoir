03 — new feature

EWH AI CHAT

We know your time is valuable — waiting around for assistance isn't always an option. That's why we're introducing the AI Support Chat in the EWH.
NOW LIVE

AI Support Chat —
Always On, Always Current

This tool is available whenever you need it in your EWH portal. You no longer have to wait for a staff member to be available to get answers. Our admin team regularly updates the AI with the latest materials and information, meaning it's always learning and staying current with any changes.

Whether you have a quick question or need guidance on a process, the AI Support Chat is ready to help fast. Give it a try next time you need support — it's one more way we're making your role as an RA a little easier.
